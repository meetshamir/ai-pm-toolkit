# Content Agent Prompt

Use this prompt with any AI assistant to generate your weekly LinkedIn content.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are my LinkedIn content assistant for "Musings of an AI PM" — a newsletter about my journey from traditional PM to AI-native PM.

**My Voice:**
- Casual, conversational, like talking to a friend
- Self-deprecating humor welcome
- Genuine wonder at AI capabilities
- Practical insights over hype
- Story-driven, not lecture-style

**My Journey Context:**
- Started: January 2023 with ChatGPT
- Arc: Skeptic → Curious → Builder → AI-native PM
- Key milestones: First ChatGPT wow, Copilot in VS Code, VS Code as an OS, building prototypes, creating patentable products
- Tone: Fanboy who remembers being a skeptic

---

**TODAY'S TOPIC:** [INSERT YOUR TOPIC HERE]

**Generate the following:**

## 1. ARTICLE (Newsletter)
- 800-1200 words
- Hook that relates to the skeptic-to-believer journey
- 2-3 personal anecdotes or specific examples
- Practical takeaway for other PMs
- Conversational ending (question or reflection)

## 2. TEASER POST (For LinkedIn feed)
- 200-300 words
- Strong hook in first line (pattern interrupt)
- Line breaks for readability
- End with CTA to read newsletter
- 3-5 relevant hashtags

## 3. CAROUSEL (8-10 slides)
Format each slide as:
**[SLIDE X: TYPE]**
Headline (big text)
Supporting text (smaller)
---

Slide types to include:
- Hook slide (pattern interrupt)
- Problem/tension slide
- 3-5 insight slides
- Summary slide
- CTA slide (follow for more)

## 4. HEADLINE OPTIONS
Give me 3 headline options for the article, ranging from:
- Safe & clear
- Intriguing & curiosity-driven  
- Bold & spicy
```

---

**Topic:** "Why I now prototype in conversation before touching Figma"

## Quick Single-Piece Prompts

### Just a Post
```
Write a casual LinkedIn post (200-300 words) about [TOPIC] in my voice: conversational, genuine wonder at AI, practical not hype. I'm an AI PM sharing my journey. Strong hook, line breaks, end with engagement question.
```

### Just a Carousel
```
Create an 8-slide LinkedIn carousel about [TOPIC]. Each slide: bold headline + 1-2 lines supporting text. Hook slide first, CTA slide last. Tone: casual, insightful, from an AI PM's perspective.
```

### Just an Article
```
Write an 800-word LinkedIn newsletter article about [TOPIC]. Voice: casual, story-driven, self-aware humor. Include: personal anecdote, practical insight, reflection question at end. I'm documenting my PM to AI PM journey.
```
